### Tickets Preparation

What:
- Acceptance criteria
- UX/UI design

How:
- Describe technical solution
- Diagrams


1.  Create a ticket 
   - Define Why
   - Define What

2.  Review and Refine the Why and What with the Architect and leads in `Pre-refinament` meeting, duration 1-2 hours
    Agenda:
    - Review Why
    - Review and Refine What
    - Define How
      - if the ticket it's complicate schedule a separate meeting with the architect/lead and the domain experts devs `tech pre-refinament` meeting
     
3. Optional `tech pre-refinament` meeting! Some tickets need to be discussed and design additionally, a meeting per ticket.
    Agenda:
    - Review Why
    - Review and Refine What
    - Define How
    Participants:
    - Product owner [optional]
    - Lead [optional]
    - Architect
    - UI/UX Designer [optional]
    - 

4. Review the ticket with team in `Refinament backlog` meeting
    Agenda:
   - Review Why
   - Review and refine What
   - Review and refine How
   
4. Sprint planing 
   - Confirm Why
   - Confirm What
   - Confirm How
   - Estimate
